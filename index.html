Features

Zero Config Required

Point Labby to your Homepage URL. It parses your HTML payload automatically. No manual entry, no duplicate YAML files. Just connect and go.

Health Monitoring

Background polling keeps an eye on your services. See what's online or offline at a glance with real-time status indicators.

A Better Browser

Dedicated WKWebView tabs with persistent cookies. Long-press to open Sonarr, manage a download, and close the tab without ever leaving the dashboard.

AI Backgrounds

Generate custom dashboard artwork with Apple Intelligence. Make your homelab feel like home.

Native support for insecure URL sessions. No more certificate warnings for local IPs. Your homelab, your certificates.
